Key Features of QUICK ASSIST

Date Gap: Calculates the gap between two dates. Users can input dates in MM/DD/YYYY format to quickly determine the interval between them.

Julian Convert: Converts Gregorian dates to Julian dates and vice versa, simplifying date conversions.

Log Assist: Drafts log notes in a standardized format with minimal input from the user. It currently supports two modes:

  • WGS Mode: Paste your log drafted from here into WGS. Draft only adjustment note through partial log button.
  • CIW Mode: Designed to prevent line breaks that hinder readability in CIW logs. Ensures that characters never break once validated in CIW.

Identifier: Auto-fetches your domain ID, so you don't need to type it again.

MLH: Either paste the date (MM/DD/YYYY) or Julian (YYDDD); it will auto convert the end result into Julian.

Letter Break: Breaks comments into multiple lines, adhering to the CIW - Request Letter's 67-character limit, ensuring clarity and readability.

Smart Log (Under Development): Provides PDR verbiage and manual FFT verbiage based on common scenarios. Includes:

  • A search option to find verbiage based on specific needs.
  • Editable comments to correct grammatical errors and enhance accuracy.
  • The ability to copy and paste comments directly.
